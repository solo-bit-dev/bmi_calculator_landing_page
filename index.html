Privacy Policy

Solobit - BMI Calculator

At Solobit, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we do and do not collect, and how your information is handled when you use the Solobit BMI Calculator mobile application.

Data Collection

Our BMI Calculator app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All calculations are performed locally on your device, and no information is sent to our servers or any third-party services.

Use of Information

The app is designed to provide you with an accurate BMI calculation based solely on the information you input (such as height and weight). This information is only used for the calculation and remains within your device.

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with any third-party services, and no external parties have access to the data you provide within the app.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and it is your responsibility to review this policy periodically.
